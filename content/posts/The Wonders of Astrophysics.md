## The Expanding Universe

### Dark Matter and Dark Energy

Although the visible matter in the universe accounts for only a small fraction, there is strong evidence for the existence of **dark matter** and **dark energy**. They are formed from the remnants of massive stars and continue to captivate scientists as they challenge our understanding of the laws of physics.
