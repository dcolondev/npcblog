## Brewing Methods

### 1. Espresso

Espresso, a full-flavored and concentrated form of coffee, is made by forcing hot water through finely ground coffee beans. It forms the basis for popular drinks like cappuccinos and lattes.

### 2. Pour Over

Pour over, also known as hand drip, is a simple yet effective brewing method. Hot water is poured over a filter containing coffee grounds, allowing the water to slowly extract the flavors and aroma.

### 3. French Press

The French press, also called a press pot, brews coffee by steeping the grounds in hot water and then pressing a plunger to separate the liquid from the coffee grounds.
